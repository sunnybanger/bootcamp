🚀 About Afterglow Health

Afterglow Health is an innovative mental health app rooted in Eastern medicine, with plans for further development into wearable technology and motivation-focused features. The app, validated through testing, integrates meditation and sound with scientific backing. It fosters a community and offers a marketplace for associated holistic wellness products/services.

🌟 Innovation Phases

Phase 1: App launch focusing on mental health with a community and marketplace.
Phase 2: Wearable chip for personalized stress monitoring and activity recommendations.
Phase 3: Motivation app combining relaxation and activity needs.

💡 Outcomes

  • Offers an alternative to existing apps with a community to counteract loneliness, especially relevant post-COVID.
  • Aims to create a culturally diverse and inclusive mental health support community.
  • Targets corporate wellness partnerships to reduce burnout and stress-related issues.

🌎 Internationalization and Growth

Positioned for easy internationalization.
Aims for 50,000 users within the first year, anticipating subsequent revenue growth and attracting investment.

💼 Operations and Finance

  • Currently using subcontractors, planning to hire FTEs as sales increase.
  • CEO leveraging marketing expertise and networking to build the community.
  • Future phases include AI and Chatbot integration.
  • Open to interns and potential KTP projects.

🚀 Market Strategy

Focus on digital marketing and storytelling to highlight the blend of Eastern wisdom and technology.
Initial model offers a lifetime deal for £17 to quickly scale the app.
Diverse pricing structures for individuals, companies, and enterprises.
